Dr. Tessy Thomas

The Missile Woman of India

Here is a list of Dr. Thomas' Top Ten Achievements:

  1. She graduated in with an engineering degree from Government Engineering College, Thrissur.
  2. She earned an M. Tech in Guided Missile from the Institute of Armament Technology, Pune, which is now known as the Defense Institute of Advanced Technology.
  3. While earning her M. Tech, Dr. Thomas designed an advanced missile system, which was one of the major movements in India, in research and defense sector.
  4. Thomas was the Associate Project Director of 3,000 km range Agni-III missile project.
  5. She was the Project Director for mission Agni-IV which was successfully tested in 2011.
  6. Dr. Thomas was appointed as the Project Director for 5,000 km range Agni-V. The missile was successfully tested on April 19, 2012.
  7. Tessy was honored with the Lal Bahadur Shastri National Award for her outstanding contribution to the field of missile technology.
  8. Dr. Thomas was awarded D.Sc.(Honoris causa) by ITM University, Gwalior in 2016
  9. The Indian Women Scientists' Association honor her with tribute, saying Tessy Thomas serves as a role model, being a mother and a scientist who is determined to achieve her dreams.
  10. She is honored with titles, such as "Angi Putri" and "Missile Woman of India"

"She is an example of a woman making her mark in a traditionally male bastion and decisively breaking the glass ceiling."

Indian Prime Minister Manimohan Singh
